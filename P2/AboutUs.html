ABOUT US

Elbes takes the lead when it comes to delivering innovative fashion.

The only retailer to stock the full ranges of C/MEO COLLECTIVE, Finders Keepers, Keepsake and The Fifth. Elbes presents a fresh selection of clothing, accessories and footwear to suit the street style master, the classicist or the eclectic visionary – and everyone in between. Offering versatile pieces that can be worn separately or matched, we invite you to create a look that is effortless, confident, and undeniably yours.

The labels offered are also a favorite of international celebrities, bloggers and street style stars, worn by Cara Delevingne, Jessica Alba, the Kardashian and Jenner sisters, Solange Knowles, Song Of Style and Margaret Zhang of Shine By Three (just to name a few). A fusion of a range of cohesive, contrasting aesthetics, ELBES’s wide selection of clothing, accessories and footwear can suit the street style master, the classicist or the eclectic visionary – and everyone in between.
