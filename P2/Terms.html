Terms & Conditions

STOCK AVAILABILITY

While ELBES makes every attempt to ensure that the online store reflects the true availability of stock, sometimes an item shown to be available online may be out of stock.
If an item you ordered is out of stock we will notify you as soon as possible and, based on your preference, either cancel and refund that particular item or your entire order.


ORDER CANCELLATION

As our orders are processed and dispatched quickly, we are unable to cancel or change any details in your order once it has been placed.
If you have any problems with your order, please contact support@elbes-store.com as soon as possible.
Please be advised that you also have the option to return your order within 14 days of receipt.


EMAIL MARKETING

For the subscription and receiving of emails, we do follow the opt in/out policy.
If anyone wants to opt out of receiving emails from us, please click the unsubscribe link included in every message.
If you are having trouble unsubscribing, please do not hesitate to contact us at support@elbes-store.com.


IMAGES

All product images seen on fashionelbes.com where the background is white and the model is headless are copyright of ELBES and under no circumstances available for use by other entities without express written consent from ELBES.
The same applies for all ELBES social media platforms.
For all enquiries regarding these images please contact fashion@elbes-store.com.


PRIVACY

ELBES is committed to protecting the personal information you provide to us.
We will only use your personal information in order to provide a service to you.
We will not share or sell this personal information with any other organizations.
However, we may disclose personal information to our service providers who assist in providing a service such as delivery or customs.


We will only use your personal information for the purpose it was intended and to provide you with information about ELBES related services that we believe you may be interested in.
If you no longer wish to receive any information or correspondence from us, please unsubscribe from our email service using the unsubscribe link or email support@elbes-store.com.
