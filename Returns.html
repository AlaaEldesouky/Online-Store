RETURNS

Returns will be accepted subject to the following conditions:

-Items are returned to ELBES within 14 days of receipt.
-Items are in original condition, with all tags intact. Items soiled with make-up, dirt, food, tanning products or similar will not be accepted.
-Footwear and eyewear are accompanied by their original box or case.
-Please note that, in the absence of any fault, returns are not available for any underwear, swimwear, earrings, hair accessories or cosmetics due to hygiene reasons.
-For full-priced items, we are happy to exchange your product for a different size in the same style as originally ordered. Due to limited availability, we are unable to exchange for different styles or products.
